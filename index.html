Sipstack.io

production ready out of the box!

Sipstack.io is an initiative to write a new breed of JVM based SIP stacks that is ops-friendly and high-performing out-of-the-box. It comes with sophisticated configuration, application metrics and operational tools, allowing you and your team to build a production ready SIP service in the shortest amount of time possible.

The Base

Any SIP stack needs a fast and robust network layer and, equally important, a fast and robust library for the framing and parsing of SIP messages. Netty.io is exactly this and is also a library that is well adopted by a large community and has been actively developed for many years. Pkts.io provides a robust library for framing and parsing many network protocols, including SIP and pkts.io was designed with SIP specifically in mind. Combine these two libraries and you will have a very solid base for a great SIP stack.

Sipstack.io is an initiative to write a new breed of SIP stacks and is based off of netty.io and pkts.io to provide the base for the stack and currently offers a stateless stack, allowing you to build your own SIP stack with performance built in!
